Laser Cut Wood Postcard

Finished product close up

May 2026

This project is a 3D rendition of my previously designed research sunburst chart, created by laser cutting wood and task board. It was made as a gift for the Vice President of Research at UVA after the initial presentation of my interactive dashboard on UVA research publication insights (see more).

The laser cutting was done at UVA's MakerSpace Scholars Lab, and all prior design work was done on Figma and Adobe Illustrator.

Tools: Figma, Adobe Illustrator, Laser Cutter, Wood, Task Board
